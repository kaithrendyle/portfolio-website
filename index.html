about

Hi, I'm Kaitlyn, a web designer and front-end dev based in Vancouver, BC. From my initial sketches to the final coded websites, I strive for style and usability in designs that work on any screen size. While I have always loved art and creating things, I did not discover my passion for web design until more recently. I have since spent all my free time poring over books and blogs in an effort to become a front-end ninja. Not only am I fluent in HTML and CSS, but I also speak Mandarin Chinese. I've lived in China and Thailand and I'm always looking to explore new places.
