about

Hi, I'm Kaitlyn, a web designer and front-end dev based in Vancouver, BC. From my initial sketches to the final coded websites, I strive for style and usability in designs that work on any screen size. I have always loved art and creating things and find that front-end development combines the two like no other. Not only am I fluent in HTML and CSS, but I also speak Mandarin Chinese. I've lived in China and Thailand and I'm always looking to explore new places.
